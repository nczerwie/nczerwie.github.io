Zhurong




Zhurong is a Chinese rover on Mars, the country's first to land on another planet after it previously landed two rovers on the Moon. The rover is part of the Tianwen-1 mission to Mars conducted by the China National Space Administration (CNSA).-https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhurong_(rover)

Zhurong's Stats

Size Mass & Weight Features
8 feet long, 10 feet wide, and 6 feet tall 530 pounds on Earth and 201 pounds on Mars Magnetometer, Ground-Penetrating Radar, Climate Station, and lots and lots of cameras

Objectives

  1. To study the characteristics of the Martian topography and geological structure.
  2. To study the characteristic of the soil on the Martian surface and the distribution of water ice.
  3. To investigate the substance composition of the Martian surface.
  4. To study the ionosphere, surface climate and environmental characteristics of Mars.
  5. To study the Martian physical fields (electromagnetic, gravitational) and internal structure.
